Wen-Jie Tseng

I am a Master Student in CS, NCTU, Hsinchu, Taiwan. My research interest is Human-Computer Interaction (HCI). Currently I am working on haptic feedbacks and tangible user interface in virtual reality. The physical size of computer is getting smaller, which makes wearable device become the next interface between human and computer. I would like to explore the novel input/output interface on human body and actuating human with the most recent techniques.
